Best Holiday Places to Go This Year

Taj Mahal This mausoleum is a magnificent embodiment of Shah Jahan's deep affection for his wife, with the finest architectural elements and ornate gemstone décor on ivory-white marble. The Taj Mahal is located on the banks of the Yamuna River and is surrounded by manicured gardens and a long fountain, both of which are picturesque destinations. The Taj Mahal, known as a wondrous marvel in the moonlight, attracts 8 million tourists each year. How to Get There You fly to India via Emirates flight from Heathrow to Delhi and travel to the Taj Mahal. You travel to Agra and back via the Yamuna Expressway in the comfort of a private vehicle. Lunch is served at a restaurant and entrance fees to Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, and Baby Taj or Mehtab Bagh are included so the details are worked out for you, you stay at the Metropolitan Hotel and Spa New Delhi is located in Connaught Place, a neighbourhood in New Delhi, and is near a metro station. Price for 1 person: 1751.75

The Colosseum The Colosseum in Rome, Italy, is a large amphitheatre that hosted events like gladiatorial games. Design Pics Inc. The Colosseum, also named the Flavian Amphitheatre, is a large amphitheatre in Rome. It was built during the reign of the Flavian emperors as a gift to the Roman people. How to get there Fly from Stansted to Italy via EasyJet and with a guide, head into the Colosseum to walk in the footsteps of gladiators, emperors, and plebeians as tales of the brutal games ring in your ears. Stroll amid the ruins of the Roman Forum, then discover layers of Roman history on Palatine Hill. Price for 1 person: £324.75

Pyramids of Giza An enormous pyramid created by the ancient Egyptians is called the Great Pyramid of Giza. It is the final remaining Wonder of the Ancient World and is located close to Cairo, Egypt. It was 147 metres tall when it was constructed, and for more than 3,800 years it held the record for highest building in the world. How to get there Fly from London Stanstead to Egypt via Lufthansa and stay at a 5 star resort with a pool and spa. Then see the pyramids of Khufu, Khafre and Menkaure; capture photos from the perfect viewpoint; ride a camel through the desert sands; then visit the great Sphinx. Price for 1 person: £688

Iran to supply Russia with drones, US warns

White house security advisor Jake Sullivan warned that Iran was preparing to supply Russian troops with drones and training on how to use said drones. This deal is certainly not impossible as the Kremlin announced that Vladimir Putin will travel to Tehran to meet with Iranian president Ebrahim Raisi.

Drones have played a crucial part within the war in Ukraine for both sides, allowing them locate enemy positions and to coordinate artillery fire. This hidden part of the war is carried out by drone intelligence units who also destroy tanks and other military assets. Surprisingly one of the most commonly used drones are the commercial off the shelf ones as they are cheap and easy to replace.

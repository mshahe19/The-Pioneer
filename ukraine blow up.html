Ukraine / July 13 2022

‘Nuke’-Kraine Strikes Back

Kiev has been striking back in the Southern Horizon region now occupied by Russia Ukraine says that its forces destroyed a Russian ammunition depot and killed more than 50 soldiers however, the Russian state media gave a different account claiming at least seven people were killed Ukraine is hailing it as a victory as it tries to drive back Russian force the Ukrainian government says this is the moment its rockets hit a Russian military depot in Nova, Kakova in Ukraine's south Russian-backed officials say U.S supplied weapons were involved in the strikes Ukraine's president says his forces are aiming to reclaim territory a DW report by Joel Dullroy exclaims the tremendous damage the Ukrainian forces upheld against the Russian army.

The Ukrainian government depicts an image of a barrage of rockets hailing down on the Russian base causes masses of destruction and a mass termination of the Russians ammunition.
